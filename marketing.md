Why is aroid different?
Other adblocking podcast apps charge based on the ads you skip, aroid doesn't because the analysis happens locally. Aroid leverages the power of your phone or computer to label and filter content, unlike other apps that leverage LLMs in data centers to label ads.


What does aroid do?
Aroid knows how ads differ from regular podcast content (this is hard and not always exact), Aroid is able to label different parts of an episode, and automatically jump over them.


Philosophy:
Host announcements about supporting the podcast are not skipped, these help keep podcats running and support the host directly without negative externalities.

Advertising promotes products that you don't need or want, it's insidious, keep it away from your ears.

Dynamic ad injection is particularly bad, the ad networks target you specifically. Your browsing data is tracked and correlated so thy can inject ads when you download an episode.

Aroid can't stop the tracking, but it can protect your ears from one more car insurance ad.

Podcasts still get paid, they served you ads after all, but you are not obligated to hear them, and you're not paying a AI company to filter out ads.
