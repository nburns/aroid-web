What Aroid does

Understands every episode

Aroid analyzes each episode to identify who is speaking and what kind of content is in each segment — interview, commentary, sponsor read, whatever it might be. That understanding drives everything else.

Navigate any episode

Speaker detection and content analysis combine to auto-generate chapters for every episode. See who's talking and what's being discussed, then jump straight there — no scrubbing required.

Skips what you don't want

Once Aroid knows what's in an episode, it can move past the parts you didn't come for. Automatically, before you even press play.

All of this runs on your device

Most apps that do any kind of audio analysis send your content to a server to do the heavy lifting. Aroid doesn't. Transcription, speaker detection, content classification — everything is computed locally, using the powerful hardware already in your pocket or on your desk.

Your audio never leaves your device. No accounts, no cloud processing, no one else's servers involved.
